Are you interested in being part of the early access group for Integration Hub?

Required

Findings:
Guide: Count how many users requested access and their main reasons for adoption7 out of 9 users are interested in early access

Guide: Identify average connection count and how often they're duplicated across productsAverage: 3-5 connections per organization; duplicated across 2-3 places
