Understanding the Analysis

Customer-Requested

Connectors explicitly requested by Nintex customers or prospects via our ideas.nintex.com platform. These address direct customer demand and can impact deal closure and retention.

Competitive Gap

Connectors supported by competitors (Cyclr, Make, OutSystems, Power Automate, Prismatic, ServiceNow, Zapier, n8n) but missing from Nintex. Closing these gaps improves our competitive positioning.

Industry Standard

Essential connectors widely supported across industries (Financial, Government, Insurance, Healthcare, and Manufacturing). Building these expands our integrations portfolio and improves platform capability in key verticals.

Competitor Coverage %

Percentage of the 8 analyzed competitors that support each connector. Higher coverage indicates stronger market demand and validates prioritization.

Priority Levels

P0 (Immediate): Critical customer requests. P1 (High): Competitive gaps with high coverage. P2 (Medium): Industry standards. P3 (Low): Emerging integrations.
