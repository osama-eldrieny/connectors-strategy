About Integrations

Integrations is Nintex's centralized platform for creating and managing all your connections to third-party applications and services. Instead of setting up the same connections repeatedly across different Nintex products and environments (Workflows, Apps, etc.), Integrations allows you to configure a connection once and reuse it everywhere. This means your team can work more efficiently, maintain consistent credentials across your entire Nintex ecosystem, and reduce the time spent on repetitive setup and maintenance tasks.
